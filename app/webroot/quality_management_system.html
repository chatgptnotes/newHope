Quality Management System

DrMHope's daily quality management system gives you the ability to identify standardise , control, and manage quality processes.
Key performance Indicators

CLINICAL INDICATORS

Infection control activities

  1. Rate of ventilator associated pneumonia(VAP)
  2. Rate of urinary catheter associated symptomatic UTI
  3. Rate of thrombophlebitis in peripheral lined patient
  4. Surgical Site infection Rate (a) Superficial      (b) Deep
  5. HIA rate - ICU & Wards
  6. Central line associated blood stream infection rate (CLABSI)
  7. Incidence of needle stick injury

Percentage of medication errors

  • Appropriate patients Assessment
    1. Time for initial assessment of indoor patients
    2. Time taken for initial assessment in emergency
  • Diagnostic services safety And quality control programs
    1. Reporting time for critical lab results
    2. Adherence to standard precaution
    3. Adherence to quality checks
  • All invasive procedures
    1. Total number surgeries performed
    2. Complication following such procedures
    3. Re exploration surgery
    • Adverse drug events
    1. No of adverse drug reaction
    2. Hypersensitivity reaction following antibiotic administration
    3. GI Bleed following NSAIDs
  • Use of anaesthesia
    1. Paraesthesia following spinal anaesthesia
    2. need of ventilation following anaesthesia
    3. Adherence to preanaesthesia assessments
    4. Total no. of anaesthesia
    5. Total no. of patients died of anaesthesia
    6. Total no. of adverse anaesthesia events

MANAGERIAL INDICATORS

Daily occupancy rate

  • Daily census like number of consultation, follow up cases, Health check Ups, Surgeries, Beside, etc, basically daily activity along with to-date number for the month
  • Average length of stay(ALOS)
  • Revenue Per Bed, per Patient , Per Doctor
  • Capacity utilization for OT, CT Scan, MIR, X-Ray, Ultrasound and other capital equipment's
  • Break up of revenue into Corporate ,Cash, Insurance and their percentage mix. Also surgical, Medical, out patient and pharmacy revenue
  • Region-wise, local, outstation, international
  • Medical specialty-wise revenue mix
  • Doctor - wise revenue
  • Inventory aging - item wise and total
  • List of inventory item nearing expiry, non moving ,slow moving etc.,
  • Discount report - summary based on the reason for discount
  • Month - on - Month or Quarter growth in Sales, Mix of Sales, Occupancy, Revenue Per bed etc
